Introduction

Who we are

Emerald Security Services Limited was incorporated in the year 2013 with the aim of bridging the gaps within Private Security Industry. Security is the key element to every business enterprise whether upcoming or existing, middle or large.

As a result of increasing security threats across the region, the current economic climate and importantly the increased awareness on personal security there is need for seamless security solutions.

  • Our vision

    To be the leading Security Solutions provider within the region

  • Our mission

    To provide Seamless Security Solutions to our clients while upholding honesty and integrity.

  • Business Objective

    To provide customer centered security solutions ranging from Protection Systems and Guarding Services which are responsive to the needs of diverse range of users.
